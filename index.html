About

Israt Jahan is a professional Urban and Regional Planner currently serving at the Urban Development Directorate (UDD), Ministry of Housing and Public Works, Government of Bangladesh. With over a decade of engagement in the planning field, she brings both academic rigour and practical expertise to her work.

Her academic foundation — a Bachelor's and Master's in Urban and Regional Planning from Jahangirnagar University — gives her a strong grounding in spatial planning theory, research methodology, and applied urban analysis.

Over the years she has gained hands-on experience across a wide range of planning assignments, working as an Urban Planning Facilitator and contributing to policy, research, and implementation at the national level.
